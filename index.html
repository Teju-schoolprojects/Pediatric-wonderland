Milk Tooth Myth Buster: Why Baby Teeth Matter

Many parents believe milk teeth don't need treatment since they fall out anyway. Here is why caring for them is essential:

  • Guiding Adult Teeth: Milk teeth act as natural space savers. Losing them early to decay causes surrounding teeth to drift, leading to severe crowding and alignment issues for adult teeth later.
  • Nutrition & Growth: Healthy primary teeth are crucial for proper chewing, helping children eat a balanced diet of vegetables, grains, and traditional foods.
  • Speech Development: Front teeth are critical for learning to pronounce words correctly, helping build confidence in early school years.
🍬

Festive Smiles: Balancing Sweets with Cavity Protection

Festivals and celebrations are full of sweet moments! We don't believe in saying 'no' to cakes, sweets, and chocolates. Instead, we offer advanced preventive shields:

  • Advanced Dental Sealants: A thin, BPA-free coating applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth, sealing deep grooves and blocking food debris and sugar.
  • Fluoride Varnish: A quick, fruit-flavored protective paint that strengthens enamel and makes teeth highly resistant to acid attacks.
  • Sweet Manners: Fun advice from our storytellers on how drinking water and rinsing after sweets keeps teeth clean and cavities away!
